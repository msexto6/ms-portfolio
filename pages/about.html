About me

I'm a hands-on Creative Director with an entrepreneurial mindset and a habit of figuring things out fast. My background covers design, video, web, AR, and experiential work—but I'm always chasing what's next. I love the pace of change and the challenge of turning ideas into something real. I've led internal teams, built campaigns for major B2B healthcare brands, and still like being in the work—whether I'm mocking up a prototype or shaping a story. As a leader, I focus on developing people—helping team members grow by connecting what they're great at with what our internal clients really need. Lately, I've been diving deeper into AI and AR—not just to move faster, but to rethink how we connect with people and bring brands to life in new ways.
